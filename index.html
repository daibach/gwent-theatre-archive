Gwent Theatre was a theatre company that employed professional actors, writers, musicians, designers and technicians using a wide range of theatrical styles to devise material of educational value for teachers, pupils and the wider community.

The company closed in 2010. This is an archive of some of the theatre's material.

Gwent Young People's Theatre (GYPT) still operates - you can find out more on their twitter account.
