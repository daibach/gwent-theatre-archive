Gwent Young People's Theatre (GYPT)

Gwent Young People's Theatre provides opportunities for young people to participate in a range of theatre activities under the direction of professional tutors.

Follow Gwent Young People's Theatre (GYPT) on twitter: @GwentYPTheatre.
